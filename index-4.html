Train with your cycle, not against it

Your menstrual cycle affects energy, strength, and recovery. Peak Fitness gives personalized recommendations for every phase so you can optimize results naturally.

🔴
Variable

Menstrual

Days 1–5. Energy varies. Recovery, gentle Pilates, or light sessions recommended.

🌱
Rising

Follicular

Days 6–13. Estrogen climbs. Time for progressive overload and hitting new PRs.

☀️
Peak

Ovulation

Day 14 ± 2. Peak strength and power output. Push the intensity.

🌙
Declining

Luteal

Days 15–28. Focus on consistency with moderate intensity or restorative yoga.
