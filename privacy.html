Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

No Pressure Just Results LLC ("we," "our," or "us") built Peak Fitness as a commercial application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Peak Fitness collects and stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your name (entered during onboarding)
  • Fitness goals, experience level, and training schedule preferences
  • Workout history including exercises, sets, reps, and weights
  • Session logs for Pilates, yoga, cardio, and other activities
  • Recovery check-in data (sleep quality, energy, stress, soreness)
  • Menstrual cycle data (if you opt in to cycle-synced training)
  • Body weight data (synced from Apple Health with your permission)

How Your Data Is Stored

All personal and workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers. Your data stays on your phone.

Apple Health Integration

With your explicit permission, Peak Fitness can read body weight data from and write workout data to Apple Health. This integration is entirely optional. You can enable or disable it at any time in the app's settings or in iOS Settings → Privacy → Health.

Subscription & Purchase Data

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information. All billing is handled by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

We may use Apple's built-in analytics to receive anonymized, aggregate data about app performance and crashes. This data does not personally identify you and is handled according to Apple's privacy practices.

Third-Party Services

Peak Fitness does not share your data with third-party advertising networks, data brokers, or analytics services beyond Apple's built-in tools. We do not sell your data to anyone.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored on your device for as long as you use the app. If you delete Peak Fitness from your device, all locally stored data is permanently removed. You can also reset your data within the app's settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

Peak Fitness is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can view, modify, or delete your data directly within the app. No request to us is needed — you own your data completely.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
